Do you know what kind of degree your child's math teacher has? It is becoming less and less likely that this teacher has a degree in mathematics. Research, however, indicates that secondary school teachers holding an undergraduate major in mathematics or a graduate degree in math was positive predictor of 10th grade student achievement. Unsurprisingly, America ranks lower than several Asian and European nations in mathematics education.

Tools For Mathematical Thinking leverages technology to develop mathematical thinking skills in Jr. High and High School students. The modules are all developed by tutors who hold at least a bachelor's degree in mathematics, and those who develop the modules also perform the podcast lessons. We do not believe that state standards for mathematics education should be followed because we believe that those standards have proven to be low.

How are you going to pay for college? Have you considered that you can make the expense considerably less by finishing on time? First time bachelor's degree students are about 35% likely to complete their degree in 4 years. The rising cost of college tuition makes the added expense of a fifth or sixth year potentially crippling.

As a product of the AP program in Texas, my personal experience in college was unpreparedness for the rigors of collegiate education. I started out poorly, and the ripple effect forced me to finish my bachelor's degree in five years instead of four. I could have avoided this expense if I were adequately prepared prior to entering college.
